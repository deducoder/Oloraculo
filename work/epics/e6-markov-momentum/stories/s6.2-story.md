# Story: S6.2: Inertia xG Modifier

## User Story
As a football prediction system,
I want to adjust a team's expected goals (xG) by up to +-10% based on their Markov transition probabilities,
so that I can factor recent momentum/inertia directly into the goals prediction model.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario: Positive momentum adjustment
Given the Markov transition win probability is high
When I predict expected goals
Then the team's expected goals (xG) is increased by up to 10%.

### Scenario: Negative momentum adjustment
Given the Markov transition loss probability is high
When I predict expected goals
Then the team's expected goals (xG) is decreased by up to 10%.

### Scenario: Degraded predictor fallback
Given the Markov predictor is degraded (e.g., due to insufficient match history)
When I predict expected goals
Then no momentum adjustment is applied.

## Examples (Specification by Example)

| Home Win Prob | Away Loss Prob | Expected Home xG Multiplier | Expected Away xG Multiplier |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.60 | 0.60 | 1.10 (Positive Adjustment) | 0.90 (Negative Adjustment) |
| Degraded | Degraded | 1.00 (No Adjustment) | 1.00 (No Adjustment) |
