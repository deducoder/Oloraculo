# Story: S6.3: Selector Momentum Bias

## User Story
As a football prediction system,
I want to apply a Momentum Bias in the final prediction selector to resolve close draw predictions,
so that I can make a more decisive and accurate prediction when one team has significantly higher momentum (inertia) or the other is collapsing.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario: Resolve close draw in favor of home team with momentum
Given the selected predictor predicts a Draw as the top pick
And the Markov momentum predictor has home win probability > 0.40 and away win probability < 0.25
When I select the final prediction
Then a Momentum Bias is applied, shifting probability from Draw to Home Win, making Home Win the new top pick (or resolving the draw).

### Scenario: Resolve close draw in favor of away team with momentum (home collapsing)
Given the selected predictor predicts a Draw as the top pick
And the Markov momentum predictor has away win probability > 0.40 and home win probability < 0.25
When I select the final prediction
Then a Momentum Bias is applied, shifting probability from Draw to Away Win, resolving the draw.

### Scenario: No bias when Markov is degraded or no strong momentum exists
Given the selected predictor predicts a Draw as the top pick
And the Markov momentum predictor is degraded or doesn't show strong momentum asymmetry
When I select the final prediction
Then no Momentum Bias is applied.

## Examples (Specification by Example)

| Selected Top Pick | Markov Home Win | Markov Away Win | Expected Final Top Pick | Momentum Bias Applied |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Draw (H: 0.30, D: 0.40, A: 0.30) | 0.60 | 0.20 | Home | Yes (Bias towards Home) |
| Draw (H: 0.30, D: 0.40, A: 0.30) | 0.20 | 0.60 | Away | Yes (Bias towards Away) |
| Draw (H: 0.30, D: 0.40, A: 0.30) | 0.33 | 0.33 | Draw | No |
