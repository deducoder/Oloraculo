# Story: S6.1: Markov Predictor Foundation

## User Story
As a football prediction system,
I want a Markov Chain momentum predictor model that calculates outcome transition probabilities,
so that I can forecast a team's next match outcome based on their recent sequence of wins, draws, and losses.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario: Calculate transition probability matrix
Given a team has match history: [W, W, D, L, L, W, D, W, L, W]
When I train the MarkovMomentumPredictor
Then I can compute the probability of a Win, Draw, or Loss for the next match given the last match was a Win.

### Scenario: Insufficient history fallback
Given a team has less than 5 matches in their history
When I request a match prediction
Then the predictor falls back to uniform probabilities (1/3 each).

## Examples (Specification by Example)

| History | Current State (Last match) | Expected Next Probabilities |
|---------|-----------------------------|----------------------------|
| W, W, W, W, W | W | Win: 100%, Draw: 0%, Loss: 0% |
| W, L, W, L, W | W | Win: 0%, Draw: 0%, Loss: 100% |
| L (under 5 matches) | L | Win: 33.3%, Draw: 33.3%, Loss: 33.3% |
